How it works

Set it once. It works quietly in the background.

No one has to change how they work. Cloasta connects to the tools you already use and builds a living map of how your company operates.

1

Connect your tools

Link Slack, Notion, Linear, and email in minutes. Cloasta reads with read-only access — it never modifies anything.

2

Knowledge is mapped automatically

Cloasta builds a structured profile for each person — their decisions, processes, key contacts, and recurring responsibilities.

Handoff when it matters

When someone is leaving, trigger a full knowledge handoff. A structured document, ready for their replacement in minutes.

Ask "how do we handle refund exceptions?" or "who owns the Stripe relationship?" — and get an answer grounded in how your company actually works.
